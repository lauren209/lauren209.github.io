About Lauren DeFrancesco

The Start

In high school I had the opportunity to travel to Europe for my birthday. It was my first real trip abroad. My dad pulled me out of school for a little over a week for us to go to Europe together. My parents split when I was in the 8th grade and traveling was a way for my dad and I to spend some time together and not want to bite each others head off.

The why behind this page

The driving factor about making this page is to share real life experiences about the trips I've taken. Now I'm sure you're thinking 'oh no not another travel blog' but this is different, more family oriented. I'm not the hot instagram model or the 30 year old who quit their job to find themselve. I'm a college student just trying to scratch off all the countries off my scratch map I have at home.
